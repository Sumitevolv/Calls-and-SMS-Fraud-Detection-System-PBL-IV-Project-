Fraud Detection

Fraudulent SMS activities can pose significant risks to users. Our system monitors transactions for signs of phishing, spoofing, and other deceptive practices. By implementing real-time alerts, we enable users to take immediate action when suspicious activity is detected.

Compliance with Regulations

We adhere to industry regulations and government acts, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and CAN-SPAM Act. Compliance ensures that our solutions not only protect users but also respect their privacy rights.

Safety Guidelines

To enhance safety in SMS communications, we encourage users to follow these guidelines: Verify Sender Information: Always confirm the identity of the sender before responding to messages. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Utilize 2FA wherever possible to add an extra layer of security. Report Suspicious Messages: Immediately report any fraudulent messages to your service provider. Educate Users: Stay informed about the latest scams and fraud tactics to recognize potential threats.
